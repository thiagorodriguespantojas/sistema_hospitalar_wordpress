# Hospital Management System: Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Hospital Management System! This quick start guide will help you get up and running with the basic features of the system.

## 1. Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser and go to [hospital-system-url].
2. Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner.
3. Enter your provided username and temporary password.
4. You will be prompted to change your password on first login.

## 2. Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard. The dashboard layout varies depending on your role (patient, doctor, nurse, or administrator).

- The main menu is located on the left side of the screen.
- Quick access buttons for common tasks are at the top of the dashboard.
- Notifications and alerts appear in the top right corner.

## 3. Key Features for All Users

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click on your name in the top right corner.
2. Select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown menu.
3. Update your information and click "Save Changes".

### Changing Your Password

1. Click on your name in the top right corner.
2. Select "Change Password" from the dropdown menu.
3. Enter your current password and your new password twice.
4. Click "Update Password".

### Sending a Message

1. Click on the "Messages" icon in the top menu.
2. Click "New Message".
3. Select the recipient(s) from the dropdown menu.
4. Type your message and click "Send".

## 4. Role-Specific Quick Start

### For Patients

- To view your medical records: Click "Medical Records" in the main menu.
- To schedule an appointment: Click "Schedule Appointment" on your dashboard.
- To view your bills: Click "Billing" in the main menu.

### For Doctors

- To view your appointments: Click "My Appointments" on your dashboard.
- To access patient records: Click "Patient Records" in the main menu and search for the patient.
- To issue a prescription: While viewing a patient's record, click "Prescribe Medication".

### For Nurses

- To update patient vitals: Click "Patient Vitals" on your dashboard and select the patient.
- To view care tasks: Click "Care Tasks" in the main menu.
- To confirm medication administration: Click "Medication Schedule" on your dashboard.

### For Administrators

- To manage user accounts: Click "User Management" in the main menu.
- To generate reports: Click "Reports" in the main menu.
- To adjust system settings: Click "System Settings" in the main menu.

## 5. Getting Help

- Click on the "Help" icon (usually a question mark) in the top right corner for context-sensitive help.
- For technical support, contact the IT helpdesk at [support-email] or [support-phone-number].

Remember to log out when you're finished using the system, especially if you're using a shared computer.

Welcome aboard, and we hope you find the Hospital Management System helpful in your daily tasks!
